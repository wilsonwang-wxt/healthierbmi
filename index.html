Methodology

Calorie Burn: Calculated using the MET formula: (MET - 1) * 0.0175 * Weight * Duration. We use "Net METs" (-1) to isolate the extra energy spent above resting levels.

Weight Loss: Assumes that a 7,700 kcal deficit leads to 1kg of fat loss.

Important Assumption: This tool assumes all other lifestyle factors remain identical, including your baseline physical activity and daily calorie intake.

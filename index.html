Methodology

Calorie Burn: Calculated using the Metabolic Equivalent of Task (MET) formula: (MET - 1) * 0.0175 * Weight * Duration. We subtract 1 MET to isolate the additional energy burned over resting levels.

Weight Loss: Based on the scientific calculation that a deficit of 7,700 kcal results in approximately 1kg of fat loss.

Important Assumption: This tool assumes all other lifestyle factors remain identical, including your baseline physical activity and daily calorie intake.

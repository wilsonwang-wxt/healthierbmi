Methodology

Calorie Burn: Calculated using the formula: (MET - 1) * 0.0175 * Weight * Duration. We subtract 1 MET to isolate the "extra" burn above resting levels.

Weight Loss: Assumes a 7,700 kcal deficit = 1kg of fat loss.

Important Note: This projection assumes all other factors remain constant, including your baseline activity and daily calorie intake.

Methodology

Calories: Uses the MET formula: (MET - 1) * 0.0175 * Weight * Time. The "-1" accounts for net calories burned above resting.

Disclaimer: This calculation assumes all other factors remain constant, including your baseline physical activity levels and average calorie intake.

Scientific Methodology

Calories: We use the Metabolic Equivalent of Task (MET) formula:
Burn = (MET - 1) * 0.0175 * Weight * Time.
We subtract 1 MET to represent the additional energy burned over resting.

Weight Loss: Based on the scientific consensus that burning 7,700 kcal extra results in roughly 1kg of fat loss.

BMI & Gender: While BMI thresholds (18.5 - 25) are globally consistent for both sexes, women typically have a higher body fat percentage at the same BMI as men. Our comparison uses Health Survey for England (HSE) 2024 benchmarks.
